What is NSS UNIT II sathy all about??

By NSS Team

share:

NSS, which stands for National Service Scheme, is a dedicated organization that works tirelessly to address the pressing needs of our environment, our people, especially children, and the broader community around us. NSS is a prominent and well-developed body, and our mission is clear - to instill a sense of "Simplicity of Life and Dignity of Labor" in all our endeavors. Our team is primarily comprised of students and mentors, all of whom actively participate in and drive our activities.

Beyond these activities, we also engage in a wide range of projects both within and outside our campus. Special events are organized on national holidays and the birth anniversaries of our national leaders to inform and inspire others to contribute to societal betterment.

Our teams have set and achieved numerous goals, working in harmony to brighten the future of children, empower women to be self-reliant, and develop villages in alignment with the vision of our nation's founding father. Each NSS volunteer is expected to complete 80 hours of service to fulfill the requirements of the course. Looking ahead, we have plans to expand our scope and include additional villages, ensuring that our impact continues to grow.
